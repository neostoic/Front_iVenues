Welcome to I-Venues

There are 4 tasks that I need you to complete and they are relatively simple. You will search for things that you like to drink and eat in the location you want and then you will have to rank 4 lists.

Each list will suggest 5 places that fit your search criteria. Based on your taste and knowledge you should position 1st the list you like the most, 2nd the second favorite, etc .

In the map you can use the Locate Me button to locate you. Also, you can drag and drop the circle in the location you want to search.

Thats it!!! Thank you for taking part in my evaluation!!
